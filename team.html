Security and Artificial Intelligence

Motivated Master's and Undergrads WANTED!

We looking for highly motivated undergraduate and Master's students to join our research team. If you are interested in working on projects in Artificial Intelligence in Games and/or Cyber Security, please send an e-mail to Dr. Siming Liu for further inquiries on research opportunities.

Involving in research projects does require significant commitment. However, it is highly rewarding and it is an excellent opportunity for personal and professional growth!

Master's students

Prospective Master's students are encouraged to contact Dr. Siming Liu and should apply to the CS Graduate Program at Missouri State University. Graduate students are expected to become capable of conducting research independently and publish at least one IEEE/ACM paper by the time of graduation.

I am an Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science at Missouri State University. My research interests are broadly in the areas of computational intelligence, artificial intelligence, and machine learning, with a focus on applications in computer games. I currently focus on using reinforcement learning and evolutionary algorithms to build AI players for a variaty of games.
